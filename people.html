Some people born in Reykjavík are famous. Here we will only name some of them.

  • Tryggvi Þórhallsson: He was prime minister of Iceland.
  • Engel Lund: She was a Danish-Icelandic Soprano

Born in the 20th century

  • Haukur Clausen: He was an Olympian athlete
  • Friðrik Ólafsson: He was a chess grandmaster
